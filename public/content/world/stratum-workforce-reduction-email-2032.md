**From:** Nathan Coles <nathan@stratum.ai>
**To:** All Employees
**Date:** August 3, 2032, 6:47 AM PT
**Subject:** A Message from Nathan

---

Team,

I'm writing to share some difficult news. Today we are reducing our workforce by approximately 1,100 roles, or roughly 18% of our global team.

This is one of the hardest decisions I've made as CEO, and I want to be transparent about why we're making it and what it means for those affected.

**Why we're doing this.**

Over the past three years, Stratum has grown from 800 to over 6,000 employees as we scaled to meet what we believed — and still believe — is a generational opportunity in AI infrastructure. That growth was necessary and it was right. But we also grew in areas where the pace of hiring outran the pace of need. Some functions were built for a trajectory we haven't yet reached. Others were built for a business model that has evolved.

The compute infrastructure market is shifting. Enterprise customers are renegotiating capacity commitments. Training workloads are consolidating among fewer, larger customers. Inference demand is growing but at lower margin than training. These are not existential challenges — they are structural adjustments, and we need to align our cost structure with where the market is, not where we projected it would be eighteen months ago.

To be direct: **we hired ahead of demand in some areas, and I own that.**

**What this means for affected employees.**

If your role has been eliminated, you will receive a separate email within the next 30 minutes from your HR business partner with specific details. For those in the US:

- **Severance:** 16 weeks of base salary, plus 1 additional week per year of service
- **Healthcare:** COBRA coverage subsidized by Stratum for 6 months
- **Equity:** Unvested RSUs on the next quarterly cliff will be accelerated. Remaining unvested RSUs will be forfeited per plan terms
- **Transition support:** 3 months of outplacement services through an external career firm
- **Laptops:** You may keep your company laptop. IT will remove company accounts and data remotely
- **References:** Managers will be available for reference calls. A standardized reference letter will be provided upon request

For employees outside the US, packages will comply with local labor law and in many cases exceed local requirements. Your regional HR team will provide details.

**Visa holders:** If your employment authorization is tied to your role at Stratum, we understand the urgency. Our immigration counsel will provide individual consultations beginning today. We are committed to supporting you through the transition period allowed under your visa category.

**What happens next for affected employees.**

Your access to Stratum systems, including email, Slack, and internal tools, will be deactivated at 9:00 AM PT today. If you have personal files on company systems, please save them before that time. Your badge access to office facilities will remain active through end of day Friday, August 5.

Exit interviews are optional and can be scheduled through your HR business partner.

**For those staying.**

I know today is painful. Many of you are losing colleagues, mentors, and friends. That loss is real, and I don't want to minimize it by pivoting immediately to what comes next.

But I do owe you clarity: Stratum's mission has not changed. The infrastructure the world needs is still being built, and we are still the ones building it. The company that emerges from this restructuring will be more focused, more efficient, and better positioned to deliver on the commitments we've made to our customers and investors.

We will hold a company-wide town hall at 2:00 PM PT today where I and the leadership team will take your questions. No topics are off limits.

I am grateful to every person who contributed to building this company. That includes the people we are saying goodbye to today. Their work built what Stratum is, and what we make of it from here is the best way to honor what they gave.

Nathan
