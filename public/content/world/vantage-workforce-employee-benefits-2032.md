## 2032 Benefits at a Glance

*For eligible full-time corporate employees (Grade L4 and above)*

---

### Health & Wellness

- **Medical, dental, vision** — 100% employer-paid premiums for employee + family (Aetna PPO, two plan options)
- **Mental health** — Unlimited therapy sessions through Lyra Health at $0 copay. In-network psychiatry fully covered.
- **Wellness stipend** — $2,400/year for gym memberships, fitness classes, massage, acupuncture, meditation apps, or wellness retreats
- **On-site amenities** (SF headquarters):
  - Full-service fitness center with personal training
  - Meditation and quiet rooms on every floor
  - Rooftop garden with guided breathwork sessions (Tuesdays and Thursdays, 12:15 PM)
  - Nap pods (Building B, 3rd floor)
  - Cold plunge and sauna (Building A, basement level)

---

### Time Off

- **Unlimited PTO** — We trust you to manage your time. No accrual, no cap. Average usage across the company in 2031 was 27 days.
- **Sabbatical** — 4 weeks paid after 4 years of service
- **Parental leave** — 20 weeks paid (birthing parent), 12 weeks paid (non-birthing parent)
- **Mental health days** — No documentation required. If you need a day, take a day.
- **Volunteering** — 3 paid volunteer days per year

---

### Financial

- **Base salary** — Corporate roles start at $125,000 (L4). See your offer letter for specifics.
- **Annual bonus** — 15–25% target based on grade and performance
- **Equity** — RSU grants at hire, refreshed annually based on performance
- **401(k)** — 6% employer match, vested immediately
- **Financial planning** — Free access to Northstar financial advisors for investment, tax, and estate planning
- **Student loan assistance** — $10,000/year toward qualifying student loan payments (max 4 years)

---

### Food & Beverage

*San Francisco headquarters:*

- Catered lunch daily (rotating cuisines, dietary accommodations available)
- Full espresso bar with two baristas (7 AM – 4 PM)
- Snack kitchens on every floor, restocked weekly — fresh fruit, trail mix, artisan granola, sparkling water, kombucha, cold brew on tap
- Monthly team dinners (company-funded, $75/person)

---

### Professional Development

- **Learning budget** — $5,000/year for courses, conferences, certifications, books
- **Internal coaching** — Matched with an executive coach after 6 months
- **Tuition reimbursement** — Up to $15,000/year for degree programs

---

### Perks

- **Commuter benefits** — $300/month pre-tax transit or $200/month parking
- **Home office stipend** — $2,000 at hire for desk, chair, monitor setup
- **Pet insurance** — Employer-subsidized through Wagmo
- **Annual company retreat** — 2031 was Carmel Valley; 2032 location TBD
- **Employee discount program** — Partnerships with Peloton, Allbirds, Warby Parker, Calm, Headspace, and 200+ brands
