### Important Reminders

- **Network matters.** All plans use the BlueCross Advantage Select network. Verify that your providers are in-network at [bluecrossadvantage.com/findcare](https://www.cms.gov/Medicare/Coding/NationalCorrectCodInitEd). Services from out-of-network providers may result in [balance billing](https://www.kff.org/health-reform/issue-brief/surprise-medical-bills/) up to the provider's full charge.

- **Prior authorization.** Certain procedures, imaging, and specialty medications require [prior authorization](https://www.ama-assn.org/practice-management/prior-authorization/prior-authorization-resources). Failure to obtain prior authorization may result in denial of coverage or reduced benefits.

- **Your deductible resets January 1.** Services received late in the year may not count toward the following year's deductible.

- **Prescription formulary.** Covered medications are listed in the [BlueCross Advantage formulary](https://www.cms.gov/Medicare/Prescription-Drug-Coverage/PrescriptionDrugCovGenIn). Medications not on the formulary require an exception request and may not be covered.

- **This plan does not include dental or vision.** Dental and vision coverage is available as a separate voluntary election at additional cost.
