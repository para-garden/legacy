**RE: Notice of Rent Adjustment — Effective September 1, 2032**

Dear Ms. Odom,

Thank you for being a valued resident at 1614 Caldwell Street. We appreciate your tenancy and hope you have enjoyed your time in the Caldwell Street community.

This letter serves as formal notice, per [Ohio Revised Code §5321.01](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-5321.01) and the terms of your month-to-month rental agreement, that your monthly rent will be adjusted as follows:

| | |
|---|---|
| **Current Monthly Rent** | $625 |
| **New Monthly Rent** | $765 |
| **Adjustment** | $140 (22.4%) |
| **Effective Date** | September 1, 2032 |
| **Notice Period** | 60 days (per lease §4.1) |

This adjustment reflects current [market conditions in the Whitmore/Summit County area](https://www.zillow.com/rental-manager/market-trends/), including increased property taxes, insurance premiums, and maintenance costs. Greystone is committed to maintaining quality housing while ensuring our properties remain competitively positioned in the local rental market.

**Your options:**

1. **Renew** at the adjusted rate. No action is required — your month-to-month tenancy will continue automatically at the new rate beginning September 1, 2032.

2. **Vacate** by August 31, 2032. Please provide written notice of intent to vacate at least 30 days prior to your move-out date. A [move-out inspection](https://codes.ohio.gov/ohio-revised-code/section-5321.16) will be scheduled, and your security deposit will be returned per Ohio law within 30 days, less any amounts owed for damages beyond normal wear and tear.

If you have questions about this adjustment, please contact our Property Services team at (614) 555-0200 or tenantservices@greystonepm.com. Our office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

We value your residency and hope you will choose to continue making 1614 Caldwell Street your home.

Warm regards,

Jennifer Tran
Regional Property Manager
Greystone Property Management
