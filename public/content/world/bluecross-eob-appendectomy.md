## HOW TO READ THIS EOB

This Explanation of Benefits is a summary of how BlueCross Advantage Select processed your claim. It is not a bill. Here's what each column means:

**Amount Billed:** The amount your provider charged. This is the starting point.

**Negotiated Discount:** The reduction in cost made possible by your provider's participation in the BlueCross Advantage Select network. This amount has been adjusted from your bill automatically — you do not owe this portion, and your provider has agreed not to bill you for it. This is one of the key benefits of using in-network providers.

**Allowed Amount:** The amount BlueCross Advantage Select has determined is the appropriate cost for this service under your plan's network agreement. This is the amount on which your cost-sharing (deductible, coinsurance, copay) is calculated.

**Plan Paid:** The amount BlueCross Advantage Select paid directly to your provider.

**Your Share:** The amount you owe your provider for this claim. Please pay your provider directly. Do not send payment to BlueCross Advantage Select.

**Reason Codes:** Codes that explain how each service line was processed. A complete list of reason codes is available at bluecrossadvantageselect.com/reasoncodes or by calling Member Services.

> If after reading this explanation you are still unsure how your benefits were applied, or if the amounts on this EOB differ from a bill you have received, please call Member Services. Our team is here to help you understand your benefits.

## IF YOU DISAGREE WITH THIS DETERMINATION

You have the right to appeal this decision. BlueCross Advantage Select is committed to a fair and transparent appeals process.

**Internal Appeal**

If you believe a claim was processed incorrectly, or that a benefit was improperly denied or reduced, you may file an internal appeal. Appeals must be submitted in writing within **180 days of the date of this EOB.** Verbal requests do not initiate the appeals process. Your written request must include: your name, Member ID, claim number(s) at issue, a description of the specific determination you are disputing, and any supporting documentation (medical records, provider statements, etc.) you wish to have considered. Submit appeals to:

> BlueCross Advantage Select — Appeals & Grievances Unit
> P.O. Box 91501
> Phoenix, AZ 85068-1501
> Fax: 1-888-204-3388

BlueCross Advantage Select will acknowledge your appeal within 5 business days and render a decision within 30 days (pre-service) or 60 days (post-service). Urgent appeals may be expedited; call Member Services to request expedited review.

**External/Independent Review**

If your internal appeal is denied, or if 60 days have passed without a decision, you may request an Independent Medical Review or External Review through your state's insurance commissioner or a federal external review organization, as applicable. **External review is available only after internal appeal rights have been exhausted**, except in cases of urgent medical necessity where expedited external review may be available concurrently.

**Your Right to Assistance**

If you need help filing an appeal, understanding this EOB, or navigating a billing dispute, you may contact your state's Consumer Assistance Program (CAP). Contact information for your state CAP is available at healthcare.gov/marketplace/appeals. BlueCross Advantage Select does not administer the Consumer Assistance Program.
