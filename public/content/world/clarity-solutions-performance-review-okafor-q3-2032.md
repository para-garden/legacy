### Qualitative Assessment

**Strengths:**

Nadia has been a consistent performer since onboarding in Q2. Her accuracy rate is well above the team median (94.1%), and her throughput places her in the top quartile of analysts on the general queue. She demonstrates strong policy comprehension and adapts quickly to mid-cycle policy updates, which have been frequent this quarter (14 updates across the review period). Her escalation judgment is sound — she escalates appropriately without over-routing, which keeps the senior reviewer queue manageable.

Nadia is reliable and professional. She has had zero unexcused absences and maintains excellent schedule adherence. She is a positive presence on the team floor.

**Areas for Development:**

Nadia's throughput on the [Graphic Media — Violence subcategory](https://www.theverge.com/2019/6/19/18681845/facebook-moderator-interviews-video-trauma-ptsd-cognizant-tampa) is slightly below her average across other categories (389 items/shift vs. 471 overall). This is within acceptable range but represents an opportunity for improvement. We recommend focused calibration sessions on the GMV decision tree to build confidence and speed in this category.

Additionally, Nadia has utilized the wellness room on 4 occasions this quarter. While wellness room access is available to all analysts and its use is encouraged, we note that the team average is 1.7 visits per quarter. This is not a performance concern at this time, but we want to ensure Nadia is aware of the full range of [wellness resources available](https://onlabor.org/theyre-looking-out-for-the-internet-but-whos-looking-out-for-them/), including the EAP (6 sessions/year — records indicate 2 used YTD).
