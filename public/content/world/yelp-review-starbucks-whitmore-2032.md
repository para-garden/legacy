**Starbucks — Whitmore University, Martin Academic Building**
★★★☆☆ · 127 reviews

---

**Alyssa T.** · Whitmore, OH
★★★★★ · October 22, 2032

Honestly my favorite Starbucks and it's not even about the coffee. I come here to study between classes and it's always the right amount of busy — like, full enough that you don't feel weird sitting alone but not so packed that you can't find a table.

The baristas are super chill. Apparently one of them used to be a student here? She always recognizes me and asks how my semester is going which is nice.

Also — there's this woman who's here literally every time I come in. She has a huge canvas bag and she sits by the window and grades papers for HOURS. She drinks like one small coffee and refills it once. I didn't realize she was a professor until my friend told me she teaches ENG 101. I guess her office is too small? Anyway she's always here and she always looks really focused and honestly it makes me feel like I should work harder lol.

One time I was having a really bad week and I was sitting at the table near her kind of stressed and she looked up and said "whatever it is, it'll be okay" and went back to grading. It was random but it actually helped?? Anyway five stars for the vibes and the mystery grading lady.

**Marcus T.** · Columbus, OH
★★★★★ · March 8, 2032

I graduated in 2027 and I was back on campus for an alumni thing and I stopped in here. The barista didn't recognize me but Dr. Odom was still at her table by the window. Same bag. Same coffee. I almost went over to say hi but she was in the middle of reading something and doing that thing she does where she writes in the margin and nods — so I just got my coffee and left.

She probably doesn't remember me. But she changed the way I think about writing. I wrote her a letter once, actual letter, actual stamp. I hope she got it.

Five stars for the coffee. Five more for the fact that some things don't change.
