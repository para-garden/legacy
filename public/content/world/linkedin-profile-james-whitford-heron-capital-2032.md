### About

I lead Heron Capital Partners, a real estate private equity firm focused on workforce housing in the Midwest and Southeast. Our thesis is simple: acquire quality housing in underserved markets, improve operations, and create value for our investors and the communities we serve. We currently manage $2.1B in AUM across three fund vintages.

Before Heron, I spent eight years at Blackstone's Real Estate group in New York, where I was involved in $4.2B of multifamily transactions. Before that, I was an analyst at Goldman Sachs.

I'm passionate about market-rate solutions to the housing affordability challenge. The best way to expand access to quality housing is to make it profitable — alignment of incentives, not charity. I've written about this for the [National Multifamily Housing Council](https://www.nmhc.org/) and spoken at the ULI Spring Meeting, NMHC Annual, and Harvard GSD's Real Estate Conference.

When I'm not thinking about cap rates, I'm probably running (3:28 marathon PR, 2030 Chicago) or coaching my kids' soccer teams.
