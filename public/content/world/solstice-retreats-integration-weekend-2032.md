## The Integration Weekend

**A three-day immersive experience in guided [ketamine-assisted therapy](https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/psychiatry/research/psychedelics-research), somatic breathwork, and intentional rest.**

You've done the work. The therapy, the journaling, the meditation apps, the podcasts. You've built the language. Now you need the space.

The Integration Weekend is designed for people who are ready to go deeper — not into crisis, but into clarity. Over three days in our Topanga Canyon sanctuary, you'll work with licensed practitioners in [a clinical framework](https://www.fda.gov/news-events/press-announcements/fda-alerts-health-care-professionals-potential-risks-associated-compounded-ketamine-products) that honors both the science and the mystery.

This is not a party. This is not a festival. This is a container — held by two psychiatrists, a somatic therapist, and a team of integration guides — for the work that can only happen when you stop.

### What's Included

**Friday Evening — Arrival & Orientation**
- Welcome circle and intention setting
- Farm-to-table dinner (organic, locally sourced, dietary accommodations honored)
- One-on-one medical screening with Dr. Lena Okafor, MD (board-certified psychiatrist)
- Cashmere robe, journal, and welcome kit in your private casita

**Saturday — The Session**
- Morning breathwork and movement (60 min)
- [Ketamine-assisted therapy session](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41591-023-02566-3) — intramuscular, clinician-administered, individually dosed
- 4-hour guided journey with live sound healing (crystal bowls, handpan, vocal toning)
- Post-session integration circle
- Restorative dinner with the cohort (max 12 participants)

**Sunday — Integration**
- Morning yoga or silent walk (your choice)
- 90-minute integration session with your assigned therapist
- Group sharing circle
- Closing ceremony and departure blessing
- Take-home integration workbook + 30-day journaling protocol
- Two follow-up virtual integration sessions (weeks 2 and 4)

### Who This Is For

The Integration Weekend is designed for adults who:

- Have an established therapeutic relationship and are looking to deepen their work
- Are interested in exploring [psychedelic-assisted therapy](https://www.hopkinsmedicine.org/psychiatry/research/psychedelics-research) in a safe, clinical, and beautiful setting
- Have the emotional resources and support systems to engage with non-ordinary states of consciousness
- Are not currently in crisis (if you are in crisis, please contact the [988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline](https://988lifeline.org/))

**Medical exclusions:** Uncontrolled hypertension, active psychosis, history of ketamine use disorder, pregnancy. Full screening completed during orientation.

### The Space

Our Topanga Canyon sanctuary sits on 12 acres of coastal sage scrub and live oak, twenty minutes from the Pacific. The property includes:

- 8 private casitas (king bed, rain shower, private patio with canyon views)
- Ceremony space with radiant-heated floor and retractable glass walls
- Salt-water plunge pool and cold plunge
- Infrared sauna
- Meditation garden with labyrinth
- Organic kitchen garden (our chef harvests daily)

We are off-grid for water (rainwater collection + well) and partially off-grid for power (solar + battery). Cell service is limited. Wi-Fi is available in the main house only. We encourage you to leave your phone in your casita.
