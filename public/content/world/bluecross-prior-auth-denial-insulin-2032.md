## DETERMINATION

**DENIED — STEP THERAPY REQUIRED**

Your prescriber has requested authorization for **Humalog (insulin lispro) KwikPen**, which is classified as a **Tier 4 (Non-Preferred Brand)** medication on the BlueCross Advantage Select formulary.

Under your plan's [step therapy protocol](https://www.ama-assn.org/delivering-care/patient-support-advocacy/prior-authorization-step-therapy), coverage for non-preferred insulin products requires documentation that the member has **tried and failed** (due to lack of efficacy or adverse reaction) at least one preferred alternative before the non-preferred product will be authorized.

## REQUIRED FOR APPEAL OR OVERRIDE

If your prescriber believes that the preferred alternatives are not appropriate for your clinical situation, they may submit a prior authorization request with the following documentation:

1. Clinical justification for medical necessity of Humalog specifically (not insulin lispro generally)
2. Documentation that the member has **tried and failed** at least one Tier 1 or Tier 2 alternative — including:
   - Name of alternative tried
   - Dates of trial (minimum 30 days required)
   - Clinical outcome (lack of efficacy, adverse reaction, or documented medical contraindication)
   - Relevant lab results (HbA1c, fasting glucose)
3. Completed [Prior Authorization Request Form — Pharmacy](https://www.cms.gov/Medicare/Prescription-Drug-Coverage/PrescriptionDrugCovGenIn) (Form PH-4200)

Submit to: BlueCross Advantage Pharmacy Services, Fax (866) 442-7201 or via the provider portal at bluecrossadvantage.com/providers.

**Determination timeline:** Standard requests are processed within [72 hours](https://www.cms.gov/Medicare/Appeals-and-Grievances/MMCAG). Urgent (expedited) requests — defined as situations where delay could seriously jeopardize the member's life, health, or ability to regain maximum function — are processed within 24 hours. Your prescriber must indicate urgency on the request form.
