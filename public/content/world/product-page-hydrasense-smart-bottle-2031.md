## How It Works

**Precision Intake Tracking.** An integrated capacitive sensor measures every sip to within ±2mL accuracy. No manual logging. No guessing. Just drink.

**Adaptive Hydration Goals.** HydraSense syncs with your fitness tracker, weather data, and activity level to calculate your optimal daily intake — personalized to you, updated in real time.

**Smart Reminders.** A gentle LED pulse reminds you to drink when you fall behind your target. Customize the frequency, color, and intensity in the app.

**Temperature Monitoring.** Know your water temperature at a glance. The integrated display shows current temp, time until optimal drinking temperature, and daily progress.

**App Integration.** Syncs via Bluetooth to the HydraSense app (iOS/Android). Track daily, weekly, and monthly hydration trends. Set goals. Earn achievements. Share your streak.

## The Science

Dehydration affects [cognitive performance](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21736786/), mood, energy, and physical output. Studies show that even mild dehydration (1–2% body weight loss) can impair concentration, increase fatigue, and reduce exercise capacity. Yet most people rely on thirst — a signal that [activates only after dehydration has already begun](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/20646222/).

HydraSense Pro closes the gap between what your body needs and what you actually drink.
