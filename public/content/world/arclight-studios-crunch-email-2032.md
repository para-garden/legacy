**From:** David Moreno <dmoreno@arclightstudios.com>
**To:** All Studio
**Date:** September 9, 2032, 4:52 PM CT
**Subject:** Project Heliograph — Schedule Update & Milestone Support

---

Hey team,

I want to give everyone a transparent update on where we stand with Heliograph and what the road to ship looks like.

As of last week, Voltan has moved our target ship date from March 14 to January 17. I know that's a significant shift, and I want to be honest with you: the work ahead of us is real. But I also want to be clear that this team has the talent, the pipeline, and the culture to deliver something exceptional on that timeline.

**What this means in practice.**

Starting Monday, September 16, we're moving into what I'm calling a **sustained milestone push**. This isn't a sprint — it's a commitment to showing up for each other and for the project during a critical window. I'm asking the team for **voluntary extended hours** through the end of the year. The expectation is that leads will work with their teams to find a [flexible schedule](https://igda.org/dss/) that supports both the milestone targets and individual needs.

To put concrete numbers to it: we need to be targeting **core hours of 9 AM to 8 PM weekdays**, with weekend availability as needed for your department's milestone deliverables. Your leads will communicate specific sprint targets. I trust every person in this studio to understand what their piece of the puzzle requires.

I want to emphasize: **this is not mandatory overtime**. But the project timeline is the project timeline, and our deliverables to Voltan are contractual. I'm confident that this team will rise to the occasion the way you always have.

**What we're doing to support you.**

We're not asking for extra effort without putting something behind it.

- **Dinner** will be provided in the break room Monday through Friday, starting the 16th. Hot meals, not pizza — we're rotating through local spots. Menus go up on the kitchen Slack channel weekly.
- **The kitchen will be fully stocked on weekends** — coffee, drinks, snacks, grab-and-go meals. If you're in the building, you're taken care of.
- **Milestone bonus:** If we hit our January 17 ship date, every full-time team member will receive a **milestone completion bonus equal to 15% of base salary**. This is on top of your standard annual bonus eligibility. I fought hard for this with Voltan, and I want you to know it's real.
- **Quiet rooms** on the second floor are available 24/7 if anyone needs a break or a nap.

**Taking care of yourselves.**

I mean this genuinely — please take care of yourselves during this push. Eat real food. Get outside. Talk to your leads if you're hitting a wall. We believe in sustainable pace, and the goal is to get through this milestone window without burning anyone out. This industry has a [long](https://ea-spouse.livejournal.com/274.html) [history](https://kotaku.com/inside-rockstar-games-culture-of-crunch-1829936466) of getting this wrong, and I don't want Arclight to be that story. If something isn't working, my door is open.

**Why this matters.**

I've been in this industry for eighteen years. I've shipped games I was proud of and games I wasn't. Heliograph is the former. What this team has built over the last two and a half years — the world, the systems, the craft in every environment and animation and line of dialogue — it's [genuinely rare](https://www.gamedeveloper.com/audio/crunch-and-extended-hours-both-down-in-latest-igda-dev-satisfaction-survey). You should be proud of it. I am.

The next four months will be hard. I won't sugarcoat that. But when this ships, when players walk through what you built and feel what you intended them to feel — that's the thing. That's why we're here.

Let's go make something people remember.

David Moreno
Studio Director, Arclight Studios
