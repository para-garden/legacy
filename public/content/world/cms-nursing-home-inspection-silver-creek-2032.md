**Survey excerpt (F-0725, Sufficient Nursing Staff):**

> *During the August 14, 2032 survey, observations and record review revealed the facility operated below minimum staffing levels on 12 of the 14 days reviewed. On the night shift of July 28, 2032, 4 CNAs were responsible for 106 residents (1:26.5 ratio). The facility's staffing plan indicates a target of 7 CNAs on the night shift (1:15 ratio). Interview with CNA #3: "We can't get to everyone. We prioritize falls. Everything else waits." Interview with the Director of Nursing: "We post positions continuously. We can't fill them."*

*[There are approximately 15,000 nursing homes in the United States](https://data.cms.gov/provider-data/topics/nursing-homes/), serving 1.2 million residents. [Approximately 82% are for-profit.](https://data.cms.gov/provider-data/topics/nursing-homes/) [A 2023 study published in JAMA Health Forum found that for-profit nursing homes had 10% more deficiencies](https://jamanetwork.com/journals/jama-health-forum) and 11% higher mortality rates than nonprofit facilities.*

*[The average annual turnover rate for CNAs in nursing homes is approximately 94%](https://www.ahcancal.org/Data-and-Research/Pages/default.aspx) — meaning that over the course of a year, nearly every CNA position turns over. [The median CNA wage in nursing homes is $16.49/hour](https://www.bls.gov/ooh/healthcare/nursing-assistants.htm). The [Amazon fulfillment center in Akron, OH, starts at $19/hour](https://www.aboutamazon.com/workplace/compensation-benefits).*

*[CMS fined Silver Creek $14,820 for the staffing deficiency that resulted in actual harm.](https://data.cms.gov/) [Meridian Care Partners reported $148 million in operating revenue across its 23 facilities in fiscal year 2031.](https://silver-creek-staffing-memo-2032) The fine represents 0.01% of operating revenue. [The national average CMS fine for a staffing deficiency resulting in actual harm is approximately $21,000.](https://data.cms.gov/) [The maximum fine under federal law is $25,813 per day of noncompliance.](https://www.cms.gov/medicare/health-safety-standards) Fines at the maximum rate are rarely imposed.*
