Dear Dr. Odom,

I am pleased to inform you that the Department of English has recommended your appointment as **Adjunct Instructor** for the Fall 2032 semester, effective August 28, 2032, through December 15, 2032.

Your teaching assignment for the fall semester is as follows:

| Course | Section | Schedule | Enrollment Cap |
|---|---|---|---|
| ENG 101: First-Year Composition | 04 | TR 11:00–12:15 | 26 |
| ENG 102: Composition & Research | 07 | TR 2:00–3:15 | 26 |
| ENG 102: Composition & Research | 11 | MW 9:30–10:45 | 26 |
| ENG 102: Composition & Research | 14 | MW 1:00–2:15 | 26 |

**Compensation:** [$3,500 per course](https://www.cupahr.org/resource/adjunct-faculty-in-the-higher-education-workforce/), totaling **$14,000** for four sections, paid in eight bi-weekly installments beginning September 13, 2032.

Please note the following terms of your appointment:

- This is a **semester-by-semester appointment** and does not constitute a continuing obligation by the University. Reappointment for subsequent terms is contingent upon departmental need, enrollment, and satisfactory performance.
- Adjunct instructors are classified as [part-time employees](https://www.irs.gov/affordable-care-act/employers/identifying-full-time-employees) and are **not eligible** for University health, dental, vision, retirement, or tuition benefits.
- Your maximum scheduled hours shall not exceed [27.5 hours per week](https://www.irs.gov/affordable-care-act/employers/identifying-full-time-employees) per University policy.
- Office space is available on a shared basis in Hargrove 412B. Please coordinate scheduling with the Department of English main office.
- You are expected to hold a minimum of two office hours per week and to be available to students by appointment.
- All course syllabi must align with the Department of English's common learning outcomes and must be submitted to the Department Chair by August 26.
- You are required to complete the University's annual compliance training modules (Title IX, FERPA, Workplace Safety) by September 15. These modules are available through Whitmore Online at whitmore.edu/compliance.

Please sign and return this letter to the Office of Academic Affairs by **August 23, 2032** to confirm your acceptance. If we do not receive your signed acceptance by this date, the appointment will be offered to another qualified instructor.

We appreciate your continued contributions to Whitmore University and wish you a productive semester.

Sincerely,

Dr. Patricia Huang
Associate Provost for Academic Affairs
Whitmore University

**ACCEPTANCE**

I accept the appointment as described above. I understand that this appointment is for the Fall 2032 semester only and does not constitute a promise of future employment.
