### Your Right to Continue Coverage

Under the [Consolidated Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act (COBRA)](https://www.dol.gov/general/topic/health-plans/cobra), you have the right to temporarily continue the group health coverage you had as an employee of Arclight Studios. This is called "continuation coverage."

You have **60 days** from the date of this notice (or the date coverage ends, whichever is later) to elect COBRA continuation coverage. If you do not elect coverage within this period, **your right to COBRA will expire and cannot be reinstated.**

---

### Alternatives to COBRA

Before electing COBRA, you may wish to explore other coverage options that may be more affordable:

- **[Healthcare.gov](https://www.healthcare.gov/)** — You may qualify for a Special Enrollment Period on the ACA Marketplace due to loss of employer coverage. Depending on your income, you may be eligible for [premium tax credits](https://www.healthcare.gov/lower-costs/) that reduce your monthly premium. You have **60 days** from your qualifying event to enroll.
- **[Medicaid](https://www.medicaid.gov/)** — If your income falls below your state's eligibility threshold, you may qualify for Medicaid at no cost. In Texas, Medicaid eligibility for non-disabled adults without dependents is [extremely limited](https://www.kff.org/medicaid/issue-brief/status-of-state-medicaid-expansion-decisions/).
- **Parent's plan** — If you are under 26, you may be eligible to enroll on a parent's health plan.
