### Financial Assistance — Ascend Cares Program

You may qualify for partial or full charity care through the **Ascend Cares Financial Assistance Program** if your household income is at or below [400% of the Federal Poverty Level](https://aspe.hhs.gov/topics/poverty-economic-mobility/poverty-guidelines).

**To apply, submit the following to Patient Financial Services within 240 days of your service date:**

- Completed Ascend Cares Application (available at ascendhealthsystem.com/financialassistance)
- Two most recent federal tax returns (all pages)
- Two most recent pay stubs for all employed household members
- Most recent three months of bank statements (all accounts)
- Proof of insurance or denial of coverage letter
- Government-issued photo ID
- Proof of address (utility bill or lease agreement dated within 60 days)
- If self-employed: most recent Schedule C and profit/loss statement
- If unemployed: documentation of unemployment benefits or signed self-attestation

Applications are reviewed within 30 business days of receipt of **complete documentation**. Incomplete applications will not be reviewed and will be returned. You may resubmit a corrected application within the 240-day window.

For assistance completing your application, contact our Financial Counseling office at (844) 501-9217.

## NOTICE OF PATIENT RIGHTS

You have the right to request an itemized statement of all charges. This document is your itemized statement. To dispute a charge, submit a written request to Patient Financial Services within 60 days of this statement date.

[Under the No Surprises Act (effective January 1, 2022), uninsured or self-pay patients may request a Good Faith Estimate before scheduled services.](https://www.cms.gov/nosurprises) Emergency services are exempt from the Good Faith Estimate requirement, as estimates cannot be provided before unscheduled care. If you received a Good Faith Estimate and the final bill exceeds it by $400 or more, [you may dispute the bill through the federal patient-provider dispute resolution process](https://www.cms.gov/files/document/gfe-and-ppdr-requirements-slides.pdf).

A copy of our Notice of Privacy Practices is available at ascendhealthsystem.com/privacy or upon request.
