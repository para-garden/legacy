Dear Ms. Park,

On behalf of Luminance Entertainment Co., Ltd. ("Luminance" or "the Company"), we are writing to inform you that your Trainee Agreement dated October 1, 2030 (the "Agreement") will be terminated effective September 30, 2031.

&nbsp;

### Background

Following an extensive internal review of the Company's [debut strategy and group composition plans](https://www.billboard.com/music/pop/k-pop-trainee-life-reality-documentary-1235640921/) for the upcoming fiscal year, the Trainee Development Division has finalized the formation of a five-member debut group under the project designation PRISM. This decision reflects the Company's assessment of market positioning, concept alignment, and [long-term commercial viability](https://respawn.outlookindia.com/pop-culture/pop-culture-guides/how-much-does-a-k-pop-group-cost-the-trainee-business-model).

As a result of this restructuring, the Company will be releasing several trainees from the current cohort whose profiles, while valued, do not align with the finalized group concept. Your trainee position is among those affected by this decision.

We want to express our sincere gratitude for your dedication and effort throughout the eleven months of your training period. Your instructors have consistently noted your strong work ethic, positive attitude, and [steady improvement across multiple performance categories](https://www.billboard.com/music/pop/k-pop-trainee-life-reality-documentary-1235640921/). These qualities will serve you well in any future endeavors you choose to pursue.

&nbsp;

### Terms of Termination

The following terms apply upon termination of the Agreement, per the provisions outlined at the time of execution:

&nbsp;

Per **Trainee Agreement §7.4**, the accumulated training investment advanced by Luminance Entertainment during the trainee period remains the [financial responsibility of the former trainee](https://www.bbc.com/news/world-asia-57264449). The total outstanding balance is as follows:

The full amount of [₩84,480,000](https://www.koreaboo.com/news/3rd-gen-kpop-idol-exposes-trainee-debt/) (approximately $63,400 USD at current exchange rates) is due and payable per the terms below.

&nbsp;

**2. Payment Arrangement**

Luminance Entertainment recognizes that the immediate repayment of the full training investment may present a financial hardship. Accordingly, the Company is prepared to offer a structured [repayment plan](https://www.allkpop.com/article/2020/10/a-trainee-ends-up-with-a-debt-of-600-million-krw-518037-usd-over-eight-years-after-being-initially-told-he-will-debut-right-away) under the following terms:

- **Repayment Period:** 36 months from the date of termination
- **Monthly Installment:** ₩2,346,667 (approximately $1,760 USD)
- **First Payment Due:** November 1, 2031
- **Interest:** 0% if all payments are received on time; delinquent payments are subject to a late fee of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance
- **Full Prepayment:** Permitted at any time without penalty

To accept this arrangement, the enclosed Payment Agreement Form (Form LUM-FIN-2031-04) must be signed and returned to the Finance Division within thirty (30) calendar days of receipt of this notice. If the Payment Agreement Form is not received within this period, the full balance of ₩84,480,000 will become immediately due and payable.

**3. Intellectual Property**

Per **Trainee Agreement §9.1**, Luminance Entertainment retains all rights, title, and interest in any musical arrangements, choreographic works, vocal recordings, video content, or other creative materials produced during the training period. This includes, but is not limited to, showcase performances, training evaluations, and any content recorded for internal or promotional use. The former trainee may not reproduce, distribute, or publicly perform any such materials without the prior written consent of Luminance Entertainment.

&nbsp;

**4. [Exclusivity and Cooling Period](https://www.koreaboo.com/stories/exposed-terms-kpop-trainee-contracts-privacy-depression/)**

Per **Trainee Agreement §11.2**, a cooling period of [twenty-four (24) months](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slave_contract) shall apply following the termination date. During this period, the former trainee may not enter into a trainee agreement, management contract, or exclusive representation arrangement with any other entertainment company, talent agency, or music label without the prior written consent of Luminance Entertainment. Requests for early release from the cooling period may be submitted in writing to the Legal Affairs Division and will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

&nbsp;

**5. Confidentiality**

Per **Trainee Agreement §12.3**, the former trainee remains bound by the confidentiality provisions of the Agreement for a period of thirty-six (36) months following termination. This includes, but is not limited to, training methodologies, internal evaluation criteria, proprietary choreography, unreleased musical works, and the identities and personal information of current trainees.

&nbsp;

**6. Return of Company Property**

All Company-issued materials, including dormitory key cards, training uniforms, dietary and fitness tracking devices, and identification badges, must be returned to the Trainee Management Division no later than the termination date. Unreturned items will be charged to the former trainee at replacement cost.

&nbsp;

### Departure Logistics

The Trainee Management Division will coordinate your departure from Company dormitory housing. Please contact Ms. Yoon Ji-eun (yoon.jieun@luminance-ent.kr) to arrange the return of personal belongings and schedule your checkout inspection no later than September 28, 2031.

If you require assistance with travel arrangements to your home address on record, the Company's travel coordinator can assist with booking. Please note that travel costs will be the responsibility of the former trainee.

&nbsp;

By signing below, the former trainee acknowledges receipt and understanding of this notice, the terms of termination outlined herein, and the continuing obligations under the Trainee Agreement.

This notice does not constitute a waiver of any rights held by Luminance Entertainment under the Agreement or applicable law.
