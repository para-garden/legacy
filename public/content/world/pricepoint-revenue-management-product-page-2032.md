## Revenue Management, Reimagined

**Stop leaving money on the table.** PricePoint AI is the multifamily industry's leading [revenue management platform](https://www.propublica.org/article/yieldstar-rent-increase-realpage-rent), trusted by operators managing over 3.8 million units across 42 states.

Our AI-powered pricing engine analyzes real-time market data — including proprietary [competitor supply and demand signals](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/justice-department-sues-realpage-algorithmic-pricing-scheme-harms-millions-american-renters) — to recommend optimal rent for every unit, every day. No guesswork. No gut calls. Just data.

---

### The Problem

Property managers are trained to fill vacancies. It's instinct: a vacant unit feels like lost revenue. So they lower rent. They offer concessions. They negotiate.

**This instinct costs you money.**

A vacant unit at the right price generates [more long-term revenue](https://www.propublica.org/article/yieldstar-rent-increase-realpage-rent) than a filled unit at the wrong one. PricePoint's algorithm understands this. Your team doesn't have to.

---

### How It Works

1. **You share your data.** Lease terms, occupancy, renewals, concessions — our platform ingests your portfolio in real time through direct integration with your property management software.

2. **We enrich it.** PricePoint aggregates [non-public market data from our operator network](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/justice-department-sues-realpage-algorithmic-pricing-scheme-harms-millions-american-renters) — occupancy trends, lease velocity, renewal rates, and pricing across comparable properties in your market. The more operators in your market who use PricePoint, the more precise our recommendations become.

3. **You get a price.** Every unit. Every day. Our algorithm generates a recommended rent based on 47 demand signals, updated in real time. New lease, renewal, month-to-month — each gets a tailored recommendation calibrated to maximize revenue per square foot.

4. **You follow the recommendation.** The system works when you trust it. PricePoint tracks [recommendation compliance](https://www.propublica.org/article/yieldstar-rent-increase-realpage-rent) across your portfolio — operators who follow our pricing achieve an average of **3.2% higher revenue growth** than those who override.

### Features

**Daily Pricing Engine**\
Real-time rent recommendations for every unit type, floor plan, and lease term. Updated continuously as market conditions shift.

**Renewal Optimization**\
Maximize renewal revenue by pricing each renewal individually based on the resident's [likelihood of turnover at each price point](https://www.propublica.org/article/yieldstar-rent-increase-realpage-rent). The algorithm identifies exactly how much you can raise rent before the resident leaves — and whether their departure would be net-positive.

**Competitor Intelligence**\
[Proprietary data from our operator network](https://www.justice.gov/opa/pr/justice-department-sues-realpage-algorithmic-pricing-scheme-harms-millions-american-renters) gives you visibility into competitor pricing, occupancy, and concessions that isn't available from any public source. The more participants in your market, the sharper the signal.

**Compliance Dashboard**\
Track how often your property managers follow vs. override recommendations. Identify underperforming sites. Set escalation thresholds for overrides above a certain dollar amount. Some clients require manager justification for any override — we support that workflow.

**Concession Control**\
The algorithm tells you when to offer concessions and when to hold firm. Most operators over-concede. PricePoint helps you stop.
