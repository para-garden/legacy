**Phi Delta Sigma Under Review Following October Complaint**

*Staff Reporter*

Phi Delta Sigma fraternity is under review by the university's Office of Community Standards following a complaint related to an off-campus social event held September 27.

The university confirmed the review in a statement Monday, saying it "takes all reports of misconduct seriously and is committed to a thorough and impartial process." The statement did not specify the nature of the complaint.

A spokesperson for Phi Delta Sigma's national office said the chapter is cooperating fully with university officials. "The safety and wellbeing of all guests at our events is a top priority," the statement read. "We are committed to working through this process and have already taken steps to strengthen our event protocols."

The chapter has not been suspended during the review. A determination is expected before the end of the fall semester.
