## K-Pop / Idol Industry

### Trainee Pipeline

Companies sign trainees at 12-13 years old. ~10% of trainees ever debut. Korea Creative Content Agency: trainee population fell from 1,895 in 2020 to 1,170 in 2022 (38.3% drop).

- [K-pop trainee rules (SCMP)](https://www.scmp.com/magazines/style/celebrity/article/3110355/k-pop-trainee-rules-no-dating-no-phones-weekly-weight)
- [Trainee numbers dropping (Korea Times)](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/entertainment/k-pop/20251201/why-teen-k-pop-trainee-numbers-are-dropping-despite-greater-investment)

### Debt

Daisy (MOMOLAND): ~$150,000 trainee debt, paid once in two years, translation job paid 1.3x more. Minjoon: ₩600M debt (~$518K), CEO billing "life lectures" at ₩1M/hour, sued for defamation when he went public.

- [Daisy trainee debt (Koreaboo)](https://www.koreaboo.com/news/3rd-gen-kpop-idol-exposes-trainee-debt/)
- [Minjoon ₩600M debt (allkpop)](https://www.allkpop.com/article/2020/10/a-trainee-ends-up-with-a-debt-of-600-million-krw-518037-usd-over-eight-years-after-being-initially-told-he-will-debut-right-away)

### Contracts

TVXQ paid ₩50,000 after 500,000 albums = ₩10,000 (~$9) per member. 2010 FTC cap of 7 years. Dating bans 3-5 years post-debut. Momo: ordered to lose 7kg in one week, ate only ice cubes. Weekly weigh-ins posted publicly.

- [TVXQ payment (Koreaboo)](https://www.koreaboo.com/stories/tvxq-legal-dispute-sm-entertainment-recap-payment-unfair/)
- [Slave contract (Wikipedia)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slave_contract)
- [K-pop trainee rules (SCMP)](https://www.scmp.com/magazines/style/celebrity/article/3110355/k-pop-trainee-rules-no-dating-no-phones-weekly-weight)

### Mental Health

Jonghyun (SHINee) died by suicide Dec 2017. VCHA member KG: "environment that encourages eating disorders and has caused members to self-harm." 42% of female idols reported extreme dieting.

- [VCHA/JYP allegations (Korea Times)](https://www.koreatimes.co.kr/entertainment/k-pop/20241209/jyp-faces-abuse-allegations-from-vchas-american-member-kg)

## Animation Industry

### Pay (Japan)

¥200/drawing (~$2), 20-30 min per drawing. JAniCA 2023: inbetween animators ¥2.63M/year (~$18K). Median 225 hours/month, 65 hours overtime. Madhouse: 400 hours in a month, 37 consecutive days no day off. KyoAni exception: full employment, ¥172K-202K/month, benefits.

- [Labor Challenges in Japan's Anime (Nippon.com)](https://www.nippon.com/en/in-depth/d01174/)
- [Pay controversy (SlashFilm)](https://www.slashfilm.com/782183/the-pay-controversy-that-plagues-the-japanese-anime-industry/)
- [Animator salaries by studio (Anime Galaxy)](https://animegalaxyofficial.com/monthly-salary-of-animators-working-in-different-animation-studios-of-japan/)

### Pay (US)

BLS median $99,060; bottom 10% under $57,090. Animation Guild 2024 contract: 7%/4%/3.5% raises, AI protections.

- [BLS Animators](https://www.bls.gov/ooh/arts-and-design/multimedia-artists-and-animators.htm)
- [Animation Guild contract (Animation Magazine)](https://www.animationmagazine.net/2024/12/animation-guild-members-ratify-new-contract/)

### Layoffs

1/3 of Animation Guild laid off by mid-2024. Pixar 175 (14%), DreamWorks 70, Netflix Animation 50 (1/3 of feature division). Production dropped 40% by Q2 2024. India outsourcing at $25/hour vs 3-4x US.

- [Pixar layoffs (Variety)](https://variety.com/2024/film/news/pixar-layoffs-175-staffers-cut-1236011766/)
- [Animation crisis (The Wrap)](https://www.thewrap.com/animation-anger-hollywood-artists-crisis-contract-talks/)

## Game Development

### Crunch

EA ea_spouse 2004, 80+ hour weeks, $30.5M in settlements. Rockstar RDR2: 60-70 hour weeks for 2 years, "culture of fear" from 6 employees. CDPR Cyberpunk: mandatory 6-day weeks, $1.85M settlement.

### Layoffs

Telltale 225 in a day, 30 min to clear desks, no severance. Tango Gameworks closed after Hi-Fi Rush (3M players, BAFTA winner). 45,000 jobs lost 2022-2025. Raven Software QA layoffs led to first game dev union.
